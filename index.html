Welcome to Interval Master!

Have a musicianship exam coming up, or just want to brush up on your aural skills?

Then Interval Master is the perfect place for you to practice your interval recognition!

How It Works:

Click on the big button shaped like beamed quavers to hear an interval.

Need to hear it again? Don't worry - just click it again.

Next Step:

Once you think you recognise the interval, select an interval quality and interval number option from the drop down menus, and press "submit"!
